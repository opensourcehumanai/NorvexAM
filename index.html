Approach

We aim to own durable businesses at sensible prices, with a repeatable research process and a portfolio construction discipline that respects uncertainty.

Quality underwriting

We build conviction through business model clarity, unit economics, competitive moats, and management incentives.

Valuation with humility

We stress test assumptions, compare against plausible alternatives, and require a margin of safety.

Risk discipline

We size positions based on conviction and downside, manage correlation, and avoid forced selling via liquidity awareness.

Strategies

Clear, focused mandates—structured for real-world implementation and communication. We can tailor guidelines based on investor objectives and constraints.

Core Equity

A concentrated, fundamentals-driven portfolio of high-quality businesses, intended for multi-year holding periods.

  • Conviction-weighted allocation, with explicit downside and scenario work.

  • Emphasis on durability, capital allocation, and incentive alignment.

  • Regular letters covering decisions, attribution, and lessons learned.

Absolute Return Mindset

A flexible framework centered on capital preservation and opportunity capture, with thoughtful exposure and liquidity planning.

  • Exposure management to reduce vulnerability in fragile regimes.

  • Opportunistic adds during volatility, guided by pre-defined research work.

  • Transparent guardrails: concentration, liquidity, and drawdown sensitivity.

Governance & transparency

Boutique doesn’t mean opaque. We prioritize clarity, measured decision-making, and strong operational discipline.

Decision journal

We document theses, risk cases, sizing rationale, and what would change our minds.

Clear reporting

Simple, consistent updates: what we did, why we did it, and what we’re watching next.

Operational rigor

We keep systems clean: data hygiene, access controls, and disciplined execution workflows.
