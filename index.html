Approach

A focused approach to modern markets.

Market structure & price behavior

Norvex Asset Management follows a discretionary trading approach focused on crypto derivatives in highly liquid markets. Investment decisions are guided by market structure and price behavior.

Continuously evaluated data

Decisions are informed by continuously evaluated data. Each position reflects a deliberate assessment of risk, timing and asymmetric opportunity.

Selective engagement

The objective is not constant activity, but selective engagement. Norvex enters markets only when conditions align with a clear edge.

Firm

Discretion, grounded in structure.

Continuity by design

Norvex is led personally, but structured for continuity under clear legal arrangements. Defined deputy roles ensure the structure remains operable when required.

  • Internal guardrails support disciplined liquidity management and orderly withdrawals.

  • Operability planning with defined deputy roles when required.

Structure and standards
